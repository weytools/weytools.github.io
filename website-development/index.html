Website Design

With our UX approach to website design, the goal is to serve the visitor with the information they came for, as fast as possible. The truth is, visiting websites is a hassle, and visitors want to get on with their day. We aim to get them in and out, without being frustrated or confused.

Knowledge Base

On the first day, we work with you to build a knowledge base for the website:

  • What are the key details about your business?
  • What are the ancillary details of your business?
  • What is your value proposition?
  • Should your visitors know your business process? Using this knowledge, we can scaffold out a simple website design that communicates these key concepts as quickly as possible.

Design Details

Well planned graphic design does more than just serve as a nice coat of paint. Used effectively, you can use graphics to set the mood and style of your business, and communicate your points even faster.

For example: icons, when used selectively, can communicate the areas of the website faster than words. When used together, they lead to a confident visitor; navigating with certainty.

Simplicity

We don’t cover your page in useless stock photos. We don’t pollute your web structure with meaningless definitions of industry terms. We don’t slap a hundred links on a single page just for SEO recognition.

Attempting to manipulate Google’s search algorithims with relentless links only hurts the visitor experience, and does very little to help reach your real target market.

We strive for a simple, clean experience. Only what the visitor needs, and in an intuitive spot to find it.

Hosting

We use smaller servers across our services, allowing us to only pay the features that are actually helpful. Because of this, we have a low-cost hosting package that includes domain registration.

Static Sites

Static sites infrequently change. We don’t add unnecessary blog posting features, unused e-stores, and clunky admin logins - and because of that we are able to serve a simple, non-changing website. This allows us to pay for servers at a static site cost, a fraction of the cost for CMS capable servers.

Active Management

If you need to change a page, a color scheme, or update some text: we are always available. Email us the change and it will be completed in 24-48 hours, or call us if it’s urgent. Active management is always free with our hosting services.

If you want to experiment with new pages, or blog posts, you always have that ability. Because of the static nature of the site structure - you wont be using a web-based word processor. Instead, you will be typing the blogpost in a raw text document using Markdown syntax. Once uploaded, it converts to the layout and theme of your website.

Here is an example of how a post looks in Markdown:

---
layout: post
title: Reimagining our Menu
date: 2020-07-30 12:00:00 -0500
permalink: /new-menu/
---
# New Menu
We are *always* striving for improvement.
Today, we are happy to announce our **new menu!**
